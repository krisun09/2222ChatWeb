Support-Send Message

1. How can I send message to my firend?

Step 1. Login to your account.

Step 2. If you don't have a friend in your friendlist, add a friend first by clicking the "Add friend" buttom.

Step 3. Enter your friend's username and send it.

Step 4. Click the buttom "choose friend to message".

Step 5. Enter your friend's username and the message that you want to send in the right of the website.

Step 6. Click the "Send" buttom and you are done!

2. How can I receive message from my friend?

Step 1. Login to your account.

Step 2. Click the bottom "choose friend to message".

Step 3. Click the buttom "Receive".

Step 4. Now you can receive the message from your friend!

3. How can I add friend to my friend list?

Step 1. Login to your account.

Step 2. Select the bottom "Add friend".

Step 3. Enter your friend's username.

Step 4. Click the "Send friend request" botttom and you have successfully add your friend into your friend list!
